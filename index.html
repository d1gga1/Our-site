Initiate project
Initial discussion that aims to understand the client's needs, the type of project, the work environment, the optimal delivery times. Together we can establish a general estimate of effort, working time and budget.
Back
Next
Requirements analysis
The analysis aims to create a business blueprint, written following interviews with key users. During the analysis, the project purpose is validated by the project managers. Based on the development specifications, are created test scenarios, development specifications, and all that must be approved by the beneficiary.
Back
Next
Plan
The role of planning is to ensure that all parameters of time, cost and quality are taken into account before starting the development. During planning, project managers together with technical teams carefully analyze development requirements, clarify all aspects and estimate the time and effort required to implement them.
Back
Next
Development
During development, our team carefully follows all specifications in the development requirements. The development process is iterative (agile), the client being constantly kept up to date with the development stage. Modification requests can be resolved in real time, ensuring all the transparency necessary to frame the project in the agreed parameters of time, cost and quality. Change requests can be resolved in real time, ensuring all necessary transparency.
Back
Next
Testing
Every functionality, module or application is tested with care before delivery. We use both manual and automatic testing to detect development issues. The testing is performed on a test medium that reproduces the production environment. The testing process is performed in a controlled environment, without negative impact on the customer.
Back
Next
Client feedback
The advantage of the Agile methodology is the permanent communication between client and the project team, throughout the development process. When all the specifications are validated, the acceptance report is signed and the solution can be migrated to the production environment.
Back
Next
Maintenance
Launching into production starts the maintenance period. We follow the operating parameters of the system. We perform periodic maintenance procedures, check and maintain backup plans, monitor the space used, data traffic and costs generated by it.
